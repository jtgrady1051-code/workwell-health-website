# WorkWell Health — SWFL Mobile DOT: SEO Playbook

Research + tactics behind the proof site, and the checklist to dominate local search after launch.

## Competitive research (July 2026)

**Who ranks for "DOT physical" searches in SWFL and what they do right:**

| Site | Why it ranks |
|---|---|
| fastcdlphysical.com (Fort Myers) | Price in the page title, city name in domain, walk-in emphasis |
| gulfcoasthealthandsafety.com (Tampa) | "$79 DOT Physical Tampa FL" — price + city in title tag |

**Patterns across all high-ranking sites:** transparent flat pricing in the title tag, "same-day medical card," "FMCSA National Registry certified," city names everywhere, and phone number as the dominant call to action.

## What's already built into the proof site

1. **Title tag**: `$100 Mobile DOT Physicals Southwest Florida | We Come to You | WorkWell Health` — price + service + geography + differentiator.
2. **Structured data (JSON-LD)**: `MedicalClinic` schema with services, prices, hours, and 22 areaServed entries; `FAQPage` schema so the FAQ can win Google's rich results / "People also ask."
3. **Keyword-mapped headings**: H1 targets "DOT physicals" + mobility; H2s cover services, cities, "what to bring to a DOT physical" — all real query patterns.
4. **City & county coverage** in visible copy (not hidden text) — Fort Myers, Cape Coral, Naples, Port Charlotte, Sarasota, etc.
5. **Core Web Vitals**: single static HTML file, one image, no frameworks — loads near-instantly on cell connections at truck stops. Page speed is a ranking factor and a conversion factor.
6. **Mobile-first UX**: sticky click-to-call bar, 54px+ tap targets, native accordions — drivers on phones are the audience.
7. **FAQ content targeting long-tail queries**: cost, duration, sleep apnea/CPAP, weekend availability, fleet service.

## Launch checklist (biggest ranking levers)

1. **Google Business Profile** — this is 70% of the battle for "near me" searches.
   - Category: "Occupational health service" + "Medical clinic".
   - Service-area business covering all 10 counties; add all services with the $100 price.
   - Post weekly (photos of the truck/van on-site help enormously).
2. **Review velocity** — ask every driver for a Google review on the spot (QR code card handed over with the certificate). 50+ reviews at 4.9★ beats any on-page tactic. Replace the sample reviews on the site with real ones.
3. **City landing pages** — add `/dot-physical-fort-myers`, `/dot-physical-cape-coral`, `/dot-physical-naples`, etc. (unique copy per page: local landmarks, truck stops, terminals served). This is how floridafirstdotphysicals.com wins multiple cities.
4. **Citations / NAP consistency** — same Name/Address/Phone on Yelp, YellowPages, BBB, Apple Maps, Bing Places, and trucking directories (TruckersReport, CDL directories, FMCSA National Registry listing).
5. **Real phone number** — replace the (239) 555-0100 placeholder everywhere (HTML + schema). Use a local 239 number, not a toll-free one (local numbers rank and convert better).
6. **Connect the form** to email/SMS (Supabase or a simple form service) and add call tracking if you want attribution.
7. **Content flywheel** — one short post/month targeting long-tails: "DOT physical requirements 2026," "Can you pass a DOT physical with high blood pressure," "Captain's license physical requirements Florida."
8. **Backlinks** — local chambers of commerce, SWFL trucking companies you serve, marina associations, BBB accreditation (like Med Mobile has in PA).
